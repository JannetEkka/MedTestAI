Telemedicine Platform Requirements

US-001: Patient Video Consultation
As a patient, I want to join a video consultation with my doctor from my home so that I can receive medical care without traveling to the clinic.

Acceptance Criteria:
- One-click join from appointment confirmation email
- Compatible with desktop and mobile browsers
- High-quality video and audio
- Screen sharing capability for reviewing documents
- Session recording with patient consent

REQ-COMP-001: HIPAA Video Security
All video consultations must be conducted over encrypted connections with end-to-end encryption to protect patient privacy.
